Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 26, 2026  ·  Developer: Ethan Cooper

Poker Dojo ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information. By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Account Information Google display name, email address, profile photo URL Identify your account for cross-device sync
Training Data Hands answered, accuracy %, ELO skill rating, daily streak, stamina Power the training experience and progress tracking
Purchase Information Subscription status (subscribed / not subscribed) Unlock premium features
Preferences Haptic feedback setting Personalize the app experience
Device Identifiers Firebase-assigned user ID Link your account to cloud-synced stats

We do not collect location data, contacts, microphone or camera input, browsing history, or any sensitive financial details beyond subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the App's training engines and features
  • To sync your progress across devices when you are signed in
  • To determine which features you have access to based on subscription status
  • To send local notifications (e.g., streak reminders, stamina refill alerts)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Push Notifications

All notifications sent by Poker Dojo are local notifications scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is transmitted to any server or third-party push service (such as Firebase Cloud Messaging). You may disable notifications at any time in your device Settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

Training data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. When you are signed in with Google, your stats are additionally synced to Firebase Firestore, which is protected by Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure. Subscription status is stored locally and is not shared with third parties beyond what is required to process your purchase through Apple.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards; however, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may reset all local training data at any time from Settings → Reset All Stats. To request deletion of your account and any cloud-synced data, contact us at the email below. We will process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Poker Dojo is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Advertising

Poker Dojo does not currently display third-party advertisements and does not share data with advertising networks for targeting purposes.

9. Do Not Track

We do not track users across third-party websites or applications for advertising purposes and do not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, as such signals are not applicable to a mobile app context.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access & Portability – Request a copy of your data
  • Correction – Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion – Request erasure of your data
  • Opt-Out of Sale – We do not sell data; no opt-out is required
  • Non-Discrimination – We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights

These rights are provided to users in the European Economic Area (GDPR), California (CCPA), Virginia (CDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Texas (TDPSA), and other applicable jurisdictions.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be indicated by an updated Effective Date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
